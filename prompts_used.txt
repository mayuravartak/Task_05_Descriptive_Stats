LLM Prompt Collection – Task 05
Syracuse University Women's Lacrosse Dataset (Public Stats)
Research Goal: Evaluate how accurately a large language model (LLM) can interpret and reason about tabular sports data.


Prompt 1
Prompt: How many games did the Syracuse Women’s Lacrosse team play this season?
Goal: Count total games (number of rows) to test basic factual understanding.
Reasoning type: Factual recall / aggregation

Prompt 2
Prompt: What was the team’s overall win percentage based on the results?
Goal: Compute wins ÷ total games × 100 to summarize outcomes.
Reasoning type: Aggregation / basic computation

Prompt 3
Prompt: On average, did Syracuse score more goals in home games or away games?
Goal: Compare mean goals_for for home (H) vs away (A) to evaluate contextual performance.
Reasoning type: Comparative descriptive statistics


Notes:
1. These prompts were executed sequentially in ChatGPT (GPT-4).
2. Each response was validated against descriptive statistics generated by stats_verification.py.
3. Evaluation results are recorded separately in llm_response_summary.txt.
